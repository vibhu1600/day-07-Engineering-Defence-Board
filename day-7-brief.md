# Day 7 Student Challenge Brief

## Day 7 - The Engineering Defense Board

You are the Engineering Judgment Candidate.

This is the final CareerForge defense.

You are not here to tell a polished story. You are here to defend how you think.

## What Is Being Tested

You must prove:

- evidence ownership
- reasoning clarity
- technical judgment
- tradeoff awareness
- AI-era engineering maturity
- self-diagnosis honesty

## How The Defense Works

Before viva, submit:

1. Final Defense Dossier
2. Engineering Judgment Snapshot

During viva:

- you make a 1-minute opening capability claim
- evaluator attacks one assigned artifact
- evaluator asks challenge questions
- evaluator tests AI/tradeoff/reasoning maturity
- you close with honest self-diagnosis

The evaluator may choose any 2 days from Day 1-Day 6.

You will not know the challenged days in advance.

## Fairness

Simple English is acceptable.

Fancy English is not rewarded.

Confidence is not evidence.

If you do not understand a question, ask the evaluator to repeat or rephrase it.

You are graded on clarity of thinking, not accent, speed, or performance style.

## Strong Defense

Strong defense:

- references actual artifacts
- explains why decisions were made
- admits mistakes clearly
- explains what changed in your thinking
- names tradeoffs
- explains AI usage honestly
- says "I do not know" when appropriate

## Weak Defense

Weak defense:

- gives generic learning statements
- cannot point to evidence
- hides behind confidence
- blames the challenge
- gives GPT-style polished answers
- cannot explain what they would change
- treats completion as capability

## Final Reminder

Capability is not what you completed.

Capability is what you can defend.
