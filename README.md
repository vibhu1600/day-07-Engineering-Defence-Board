# CareerForge Day 7 - The Engineering Defense Board

Product: CareerForge Capability Defense

Role: Engineering Judgment Candidate

Core lesson:

> Capability is not what you completed. Capability is what you can defend.

## Why Day 7 Exists

Day 1 tested debugging.

Day 2 tested systems reasoning.

Day 3 tested readiness verification.

Day 4 tested ambiguity reduction.

Day 5 tested AI trust judgment.

Day 6 tested prioritization under pressure.

Day 7 tests whether you can defend your engineering judgment with evidence.

## What This Is

This is an engineering judgment defense.

It is not:

- a final presentation
- a mock interview
- a motivational closing
- a participation ceremony
- a fake certification moment

You are defending actual work from Days 1-6.

## Viva Rule

You will not know which days will be challenged.

Evaluator may choose any 2 days from Day 1-Day 6.

## Fairness Rule

Simple English is acceptable.

Fancy language is not rewarded.

Clear thinking, evidence, and honest ownership matter.

## Outcome Tiers

- Strong Pass
- Pass
- Revision Required
- Incomplete

There is no universal completion praise. Your outcome depends on evidence and defense.
